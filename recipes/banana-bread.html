Banana Bread

Description

This recipe will make a very delicious and moist banana bread. It will have everyone raving and asking for the recipe. The secret ingedient? Sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 6 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chopped walnutes (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Grease 4 7x3 inch loaf pans. In a small bowl, stir together 1/4 cup white sugar with 1 teaspoon cinnamon. Dust pans lightly with cinnamon and sugar mixture.
  2. In a large bowl, cream butter and 3 cups sugar. Mix in eggs, mashed bananas, sour cream, vanilla, and cinnamon. Mix in salt, baking soda, and flour. Stir in nuts if desired. Divide into prepared pans.
  3. Bake for 1 hour, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
